# Dream 50

A focused list of 50 target Discord communities for Guildest — the servers where an AI community operator (live pulse, alerts, server map, ask-guildest, action drafts) would create the most value, and whose owners are the most likely to pay for it.

Selection rules:

- **Large enough that manual triage breaks down.** 10k+ members or sustained daily activity in dozens of channels.
- **Owner has a commercial reason to care.** Paid product, token, course, agency, studio, or sponsor revenue tied to community health.
- **Discord is the primary surface**, not a side channel to Slack/forum/Reddit.
- **Reachable.** Public owner, founder, or community lead with a visible footprint (X, LinkedIn, podcast, newsletter).

Each row: segment, server, why-they-fit, and the hook to lead with.

## How to work the list

1. **Cluster by segment, not by row.** Run one segment at a time so the messaging compounds.
2. **Reach the owner, not the mod.** Mods like analytics; owners pay for them.
3. **Lead with a single hook from the table.** Don't pitch the platform — pitch the one thing that solves their loudest pain.
4. **Track touches.** A Dream-50 list is a 12–18 month effort, not a sprint. Five touches per target before you give up.
5. **Replace from the bench.** When a target says no or churns out of the ICP, swap in from the next-tier list. The number stays at 50.
