## Frequently Asked Questions

### How are AI reports counted?

AI reports are generated when you request a sentiment analysis report for a specific day. Each report analyzes your server's messages and provides insights about community mood, trending topics, and recommendations.

### Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my limits?

- **Server limit**: You won't be able to connect additional servers until you upgrade
- **AI reports**: Once you reach your monthly limit, you'll need to wait until next month or upgrade
- **Data retention**: Older data will be automatically pruned based on your plan's retention period

### Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund.

### Is there a trial period?

Free plan is available forever with no credit card required. Paid plans offer a 7-day free trial to test premium features.
